
# Sales Performance Dashboard Analysis using Ms Excel

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to develop an interactive Sales Dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor and analyze sales performance across different regions, product categories, and years. The dashboard provides key business metrics such as total sales, profit, quantity sold, and order count while allowing users to filter data by region, sub-category, and time period. The project aims to identify sales trends, evaluate profitability, compare product performance, and support data-driven decision-making to improve business growth and operational efficiency.

### Process 

# Data Preparation
- Collected sales data, cleaned missing values, standardized formats, and prepared the dataset for analysis.

## Project Insights
- **Sales showed consistent growth from 2014 to 2017**, increasing from approximately ₹4.84 lakh in 2014 to ₹7.33 lakh in 2017, indicating strong business expansion over the years.
- **Profit increased alongside sales**, reaching its highest value in 2017 (₹93,318), which suggests improved profitability and effective business performance.
- **Sub-categories such as Chairs, Phones, and Binders generated high sales**, while categories like Tables and Bookcases recorded negative profits, highlighting areas that require cost optimization and pricing strategy improvements.

## Final Conclusion

The Sales Dashboard successfully provides a comprehensive view of business performance by analyzing key metrics such as Orders, Quantity, Sales, and Profit. The analysis revealed a steady increase in sales and profit from 2014 to 2017, demonstrating positive business growth. High-performing sub-categories contributed significantly to revenue, while some categories generated losses, indicating opportunities for strategic improvement. By incorporating interactive filters and visualizations, the dashboard enables users to monitor performance, identify trends, evaluate profitability, and make informed business decisions. Overall, the dashboard serves as an effective tool for transforming raw sales data into actionable insights that support business growth and operational efficiency.
